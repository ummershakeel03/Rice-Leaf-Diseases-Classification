%% Important Note %%%
The repository has been extended with additional architectures, namely EfficientNetV2L and MobileNetV2, alongside previously implemented models such as InceptionV3, DenseNet201, and ResNet152V2. For the earlier models, pre-trained best-performing `.keras` weight files are utilized to ensure consistency and reproducibility in evaluation. These models are further integrated into the prediction pipeline for GrabCut-based segmentation and contour detection to refine disease region localization and comparative analysis.
